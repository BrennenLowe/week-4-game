Match the Currency!

You will be given a random target currency number at the start of the game.

There are four currencies below. By clicking on a currency you will add a specific amount of money to your total currency.

You win the game by matching your total currency to the exact target currency number. You lose the game if your total currency goes above the exact target currency.

The value of each currency is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each currency.
